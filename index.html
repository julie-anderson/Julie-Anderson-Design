Handmade pottery for everyday use.

Julie Anderson Design is the pottery practice of Chicago artist Julie Anderson, creating small-batch ceramic work rooted in texture, craft, and everyday use.

Julie’s handbuilt pottery is designed with a strong point of view and made with care. Soft, atmospheric glazes and sculptural details make functional objects feel personal, tactile, and quietly distinctive.

What I make

Handmade pottery with a distinct point of view.

Based in Chicago, Julie Anderson Design creates handmade pottery, ceramic mugs, and small-batch functional ceramics grounded in surface, form, and an appreciation for handmade variation.

Pottery

Handbuilt and small-batch ceramic work, with a focus on mugs and everyday vessels. The pottery is defined by soft, layered glazes, tactile surfaces, and a signature scalloped detail that gives each piece a gentle graphic rhythm.

Details

Julie Anderson’s pottery balances functional form with expressive surface. Each piece is made to be used and lived with, while still carrying the character of a handmade object.

About Julie Anderson Design

Julie Anderson is a Chicago artist making handmade pottery.

Julie Anderson Design brings together a love of tactile materials, clear form, and handmade process. The studio’s pottery feels intentional, grounded, and made to be lived with, balancing functional form and expressive surface.
